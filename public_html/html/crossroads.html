How to Play - Crossroads

With the Crossroads expansion, all the regular rules are the same, but these extra elements are added

6 extra travellers are added to the possible choices

Jirocho the Yakuza

At each intermediate Inn you may bet 1 coin in the Gaming Room

Daigoro the Kid

Receive one Souvenir at each intermediate Inn

Nampo the Gourmet

Each meal gives 1 additional point per coin it costs

Gotozaemon the Souvenir Seller

Gain 1 coin at every Panorama space

Miyataka the Superstitious Woman

You may donate and buy an Amulet at the same Temple

Kita the Old Woman

You may draw an Encounter card and buy a Calligraphy card at the same Encounter space

New Cards

Legendary Object cards can be bought, instead of a Souvenir, for 1, 2 or 3 coins, and provide many points in different ways. Legendary Objects count when determining who wins the Collector Achievement card

Shodo

If there are Cherry Tree cards available, you may take one instead of a Panorama card. Cherry Tree cards are worth 1 coin and 2 points.

A Bathouse card may be purchased for 1 coin instead of drawing a Hot Springs card. Bathouse cards are worth 4 points, and count towards earning the Bather Achievement card.

Coins may be bet at the Gaming Room instead of simply collected when visiting a Farm. Gamble 2 coins from your collection by rolling the Fortune Die. Lose it all or gain up to quadruple your bet, depending on what the die shows.

Instead of donating to the Temple, you may pay 1 coin to buy an Amulet card of your choice. Each Amulet card has a different, one-time use effect. After being used, it is returned to the pile of available cards.

Vitality

You may move again if your previous move put you in the lead

Fortune

Roll the Fortune Die and recieve as many coins as the number shown

Health

Take both actions of the space you land on

Friendship

You may move to a single space that is already occupied

Hospitality

Take your Meal card for free at an Inn

Devotion

When purchasing a card, spent coins are donated to the Temple

Calligraphy cards may be purchased for 1 coin instead of drawing an Encounter card. Calligraphy cards provide points at the end of your Journey in various ways.

Foresight

Each unspent coin is worth 2 points

Contemplation

Earn 1 point for each Cherry Tree card, and 3 points per completed Panorama

Nostalgia

Earn 1 point per Legendary Object card, and 1 for each Souvenir

Patience

Earn 6 points for arriving at Edo last, 4 for arriving second last, or 2 otherwise

Perfection

Earn 1 point for each Calligraphy card, and 2 for each Achievement card

Fasting

Earn 3 points for each uneaten Meal
